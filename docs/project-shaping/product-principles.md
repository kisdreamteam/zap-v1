## Teacher Control Philosophy

> **The teacher must always be able to rescue the lesson.**

### Workspace Controls (V1)

During normal activity in Story Workspace, V1 includes:

* regenerate choices
* custom choice
* end story (AI ending or manual ending)
* safe/balanced/wild mode

The teacher should never feel trapped by the AI.

### Failure Screen (V1)

When AI generation fails, the failure screen shows **Try Again** only.

Regenerate choices and custom choice are workspace controls — not offered on the failure screen in V1.

### Future

Additional failure-screen recovery paths may be added after workflow validation.

This implements [product-boundaries.md](Phase%20A/product-boundaries.md) Rule 2 — Teacher Authority Is Required.

## AI Role

The AI is not the boss.

The AI is a **co-author**.

The ideal balance:

> Students provide ideas.
> Teacher facilitates.
> AI keeps the story moving.

Story ownership should be shared between:

* students
* teacher
* AI

This implements [product-boundaries.md](Phase%20A/product-boundaries.md) Rule 3 — AI Is A Creative Guide.
