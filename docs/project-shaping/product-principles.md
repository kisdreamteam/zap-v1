# Product Principles

> V1 operational principles for Zap!. Definitional rules (teacher authority, AI role) are in [Phase A/product-boundaries.md](Phase%20A/product-boundaries.md) §3. This doc covers **V1 rescue controls**.
>
> Related: [Phase A/problem-definition.md](Phase%20A/problem-definition.md)

---

## Teacher Control Philosophy

> **The teacher must always be able to rescue the lesson.**

That means V1 includes:

* custom choice (always on main story workspace)
* on-demand plot ideas with regenerate in overlay
* on-demand discussion prompts with regenerate in overlay (in-session and whole-story contexts)
* on-demand comprehension questions with regenerate in overlay (in-session and whole-story contexts)
* end story
* safe/balanced/wild mode

The teacher should never feel trapped by the AI.

This implements [product-boundaries.md](Phase%20A/product-boundaries.md) Rule 2 — Teacher Authority Is Required.

---

## AI Role

The AI is not the boss.

The AI is a **co-author**.

The ideal balance:

> Students provide ideas.
> Teacher facilitates.
> AI keeps the story moving.

Story ownership should be shared between:

* students
* teacher
* AI

This implements [product-boundaries.md](Phase%20A/product-boundaries.md) Rule 3 — AI Is A Creative Guide.
